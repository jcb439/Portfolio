AirBend

Design Studio (2025)

Sustainable Systems Physical Interaction & Sensing

AirBend is a passive-cooling wearable that enhances thermal comfort through the body's natural movement, offering a low-power alternative to traditional cooling devices. Using fabric-based valves, integrated sensors for airflow mapping data, and computational optimization, the project leverages bioinspired mechanisms that maximizes airflow while maintaining comfort and wearability.

BuzzFinder

Design for Physical Interaction (2025)

Physical Interaction & Sensing

BuzzFinder is a wearable assistive device exploring how AI can extend the capabilities of the visually impaired while navigating an appointment or blindness when sensing nearby objects or directions. Ultrasonic sensors embedded in glasses detect surrounding objects, while vibrations inform the wearer—a necklace translates distance into intuitive, real-time feedback.

r.Joint

Redesigning Timber Joinery (2024)

Computational & Parametric Design

The r joint is a modular connection system designed for prefabricated construction featuring snapping teeth that compress into position and cantilever clips on an engraved surface. A novel design that uses standardized and optimized with calibrated speeds and material properties to facilitate CNC fabrication into an extant timber part.

OrganizeIt!

Coding For Design (2024)

Computational & Parametric Design

Organize It is a closet design tool built in Grasshopper that allows users to input their closet dimensions and desired storage configuration through visual sliders and straightforward controls. The platform demonstrates exploitation of rectangular packing algorithms and standard building dimensions to automate the process of closet design by offering personalized, space-optimized layouts. This application aims at the democratization of access to personalized and quality of traditional interior design services.
